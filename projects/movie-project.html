Movie Project

## Context

Throughout the months of learning HTML and CSS, we tested our knowledge on these codings by creating a movie webpage that consist of HTML and CSS. Every students had to choose a genre of a movie to create a movie. I chose horror/zombie apocalypse genre. We started coding our webpage on pickcode.io and had around a week to finish before turning it in.

## Context

What I made a scary looking base of background, and styled the fonts to make it look like a horror movie, then started writing the story of how it started and if they `survivors` will survive. I added images of zombies and buildings that were destroyed, and then made a sequel that connects to the movie, or continuing the cliffhanger to end with a suspense.

## Reflection

While making the webpage, I went through some troubles of getting the CSS style and HTML to work. But I still overcomed the obstacle by looking back to my notes. For the CSS styling in a new file, I didn't have the CSS link style sheet on that file so it didn't work. I asked for help and my teacher gave me one hint, but didn't tell me excatly what I was missing, and then I new what I was missing. A quick reminder that your notes and asking the teacher for help really helps! My next step would to write it down in my notes, or practice with CSS styling in another file.
